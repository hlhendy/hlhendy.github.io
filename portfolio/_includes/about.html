About

Heather is an experienced professional with 15 years of experience in both the non-profit and tech sectors. She worked for close to 10 years as a program manager at a variety of non-profits before changing careers into Software Engineering. Her software engineering career has spanned both management and data analysis. She has also helped teach a Data Analytics bootcamp as a Teacher's Assistant.

Heather holds an MA in Education Policy from The George Washington University in Washington, D.C. She has a BA in Sociology from UCLA and started a second BA in Computer Science at George Mason University, before accepting a full-time software engineer position. She is currently working on a graduate certificate in Data Science at the Colorado School of Mines.
